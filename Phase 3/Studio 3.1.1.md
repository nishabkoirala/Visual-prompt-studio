Prompt 2

Historical Theme: Humanism and the move toward scienceurpose

Historical Period: Early Renaissance(15th century Itay)

Art Work: Masaccio, Trinity

Medium Fresco painting

Visual prompt

Create a wall painting of a religious scene inside a realistic architectural space. Use linear perspective so that all lines lead into one point in the center, creating depth. The setting should include arches and columns inspired by classical Roman architecture.
In the center, create a crucified figure, with other figures standing calmly beside and behind. Below them, include two smaller figures kneeling. At the bottom, add a skeleton lying in the tomb.
The figures should look realistic, with natural body shapes and soft shading. The drapery of the clothing should fall naturally. Use balanced composition and calm colors. The scene should feel stable and organized, with a clear sense of space. 

Historical Purpose

The painting shows how early Renaissance artists were facilitated by realism, science and humanism. The perspective in a painting demonstrates an interest in math and how things appear.
The artwork was created for church to communicate religious messages more clearly to people. Naturalism makes it easier for the viewer to relate to the scene. 
It also reflects humanism when people started to prioritize knowledge. Art like this was commissioned by the church and wealthy patrons and shows the role power in the world. The skeleton symbolizes life and death, linked religion with the human condition.
